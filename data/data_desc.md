# Dataset dir

This folder contains all datasets used in the analysis for our report. The data support our visual data story **“Understanding the German Housing Market (1964–2024)”** and were selected to combine city level housing prices with key macroeconomic indicators.

---

## Contents

### 1. `GREIX_all_cities_data.xlsx`  
**Source**: German Real Estate Index (GREIX)
**Description**:  
Quality real housing prices for apartments, single-family houses, and multi-family houses across 20 German cities. The data are based on notarized transaction records and span the period from 1964 to 2024.  
**Link**: https://www.ifw-kiel.de/topics/data/greix-german-real-estate-index-4714/

---

### 2. `GDP 1960-2024.csv`  
**Source**: World Bank  
**Description**:  
Annual real GDP for Germany, covering 1960–2024. The series provides macroeconomic context for long run housing market developments.  
**Link**: https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/NY.GDP.MKTP.KD?locations=DE

---

### 3. `Germany annual average unemployment rate 2005-2025.xlsx`  
**Source**: Statistisches Bundesamt (Destatis)  
**Description**:  
Annual average unemployment rates for Germany from 2005 to 2024. The data are used to explore links between labor market conditions and housing price dynamics.  
**Link**: https://www.destatis.de/EN/Themes/Labour/Labour-Market.html

---

### 4. `Long term interest rate-Germany.csv`  
**Source**: Eurostat  
**Description**:  
Long term interest rates based on 10 year government bond yields for Germany, available from 1991 to 2025. The series is used to examine the role of financing conditions in housing market trends.  
**Link**: https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/databrowser/view/irt_lt_mcby_m/default/table
